Amankaya Plan

The Amankaya plan is one of the best for university students because it takes you to visit a festival in the province of Chiriqui.
In addition, as an extra excursion, you will visit the Panama Jungle Wildlife Refuge, and if you prefer, you can also do your social work.
Our plan includes transportation and a hostel at a cost of 100 dollars. In short, with our plan, you will be able to visit the province of Chiriqui, making it one of the best options for university students to travel.
